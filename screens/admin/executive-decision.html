A Modular Framework for Coastal Resilience in West Africa

Sector: Sustainable Infrastructure ID: 2026-ARC-0882

I. Executive Summary

This research proposal outlines a multi-scalar approach to addressing urban resilience in coastal West African corridors by integrating modular infrastructure design with longitudinal climate modeling.

Primary Objective

Establish a scalable framework for municipalities facing rising sea levels and increased storm surge frequency.

Scale of Application

Regional (West African Coastal Corridor), focusing on high-density urban zones.

II. Strategic Impact

The proposed research utilizes a mixed-methods approach, combining quantitative hydrological modeling with qualitative community stakeholder engagement. Modular Drupal-based data collection tools will be developed to facilitate real-time reporting from field sensors and mobile units.
