A Modular Framework for Coastal Resilience in West Africa

Category: PhD Research Grant

I. Executive Summary

This research proposal outlines a multi-scalar approach to addressing urban resilience in coastal West African corridors. By integrating modular infrastructure design with longitudinal climate modeling, the study aims to provide a scalable framework for municipalities facing rising sea levels and increased storm surge frequency.

II. Methodology

The proposed research utilizes a mixed-methods approach, combining quantitative hydrological modeling with qualitative community stakeholder engagement. Modular data collection tools will be developed to facilitate real-time reporting from field sensors.
