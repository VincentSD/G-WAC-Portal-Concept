Module 2: Ethics in Research

Estimated reading time: 15 mins

This module covers the fundamental principles of ethical research, including informed consent, data privacy, and institutional review board (IRB) protocols. Ethical guidelines ensure that research is conducted with integrity and respect for participants.

When processing participant data, ensure you follow the standard anonymization protocols as defined in the institute's handbook. Below is the standard data schema for participant records:
