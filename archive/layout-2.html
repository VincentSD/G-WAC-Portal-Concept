Course Overview

This course provides an intensive exploration into the frontier of synthetic biology. Students will engage with advanced genetic engineering techniques, computational modeling of biological systems, and the ethical implications of bio-innovation.

Designed for graduates in biology, chemistry, or engineering, the curriculum bridges the gap between theoretical molecular biology and practical engineering applications.
